Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Ivoria Learning Academy, we understand the importance of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and manage your data to provide you with a safe and enjoyable experience.

What Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to deliver our services and improve your experience. This may include:
  • Contact details (such as name, email, phone number)
  • Address for delivery and communication
  • Order and payment information
  • Details provided in forms, messages, or during account creation
  • Feedback, reviews, or survey responses

How We Use Your Data

Your information helps us to:
  • Process orders and deliver products or services
  • Respond to your inquiries or requests
  • Send important updates about your order or our services
  • Personalize your experience and improve our offerings
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform

Data Protection and Security

We take the security of your personal information very seriously. We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.
  • Data is stored securely and access is limited to authorized personnel only
  • We never sell or share your information with third parties except as required to fulfill your order or by law
  • We regularly review our security practices to ensure your data remains protected

Your Choices and Rights

You have full control over your personal information. You may:
  • Request corrections or updates to your details at any time
  • Ask us to delete your information (subject to legal and business needs)
  • Opt out of marketing or promotional communications
  • Contact us for any privacy-related concerns

Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting the privacy of minors. Our services are intended for learners and their families. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information.
